About Taylor Swift
Early Life

Taylor Alison Swift was born on December 13, 1989, in West Reading, Pennsylvania. She showed an early passion for musical theatre and performing arts, moving to Nashville at 14 to pursue a career in country music.

Career

Taylor signed with Big Machine Records at age 14, becoming one of the youngest artists to sign with a major Nashville label. Her self-titled debut album (2006) launched her into stardom. She has since released 11 studio albums, each defining a distinct artistic era.

Awards & Recognition

Taylor has won 14 Grammy Awards including four Album of the Year awards — the most by any artist in history. She is also the first artist to win the Grammy for Album of the Year four times.

The Eras Tour

The Eras Tour (2023–2024) became the highest-grossing concert tour in history, generating over $1 billion in revenue and breaking countless records worldwide.
